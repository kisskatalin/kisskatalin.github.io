I am a front-end web developer and UX/UI designer from Hungary.

Web development has been my hobby since I was eleven when I created my first, not too professional website. That's where I started getting into web development, and that's why I decided to go to IT secondary school and IT college years later. I was always interested in user interface design next to coding because I always made my own web designs, but this desire of mine got intense later.

While studying Business IT at college, I noticed how much the design field had grown and I discovered it by reading design books, joining design classes and finding a design mentor for my thesis. After graduating from college, I worked as a front-end web developer. User analysis shortly became a part of my job and my curiosity encouraged me to go deeper into design. Day by day, I read more articles on UX and UI, drew more sketches of wireframes and spent more times using graphics tools, eventually I found my love for pixels and my thirst of knowledge has not stopped since.
