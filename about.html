I am a UX/UI designer with coding skills.
I worked as a front-end web developer, but I switched to the creative field of design.

Web development has been my hobby since I was eleven, that's why I decided to go to college where I graduated in Business Information Technology. I was always interested in user interface design next to coding, but this desire of mine got intense later. While working as a front-end web developer, user analysis shortly became a part of the process and my curiosity encouraged me to discover the design field. Day by day, I read more articles about UX/UI, drew more sketches of wireframes and spent more times using graphics tools, eventually I found my love for pixels and my thirst of knowledge has not stopped since. I'm ready for new challenges, my growing portfolio is the best example for that, so let my works speak for themselves instead.
