Last.fm

Last.fm is a music website that provides you to scrobble tracks from music services where you listen to music. Last.fm creates recommendations, reports and statistics based on your listening history. You can love your favorite tracks, view your personal songs, artists, albums.

Problem Overview

I was interested in redesigning the current mobile application which got negative reviews from the users: it has an average 2.8 score from 78,555 users (as of April 2021) on Google Play, the most reviewers gave it 1.

Research

I asked five participants to find out what they need and how they use the mobile application. Sixty-five participants answered about what they use on Last.fm through survey. All the info given helped me to fix and improve the mobile application.

User Research Plan

I want to learn about how the users use the current mobile application and why the majority finds it unsatisfying. I want to find out what users need in the mobile application to improve it for a redesign.
